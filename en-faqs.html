Frequent Questions

Surf Lesson

 add

Portugal, in general, and Ericeira in particular is one of the best spots for surfing. Ericeira is suitable for both beginners and more experienced surfers. It is possible to surf all year round in Portugal, regardless of your surfing level. Summer offers smaller, more beginner-friendly waves. And winter offers bigger and more powerful waves, which is perfect for advanced surfers and confident intermediate students. However, if you are a beginner, we will find a suitable spot for you and you can enjoy winter surfing in Portugal. If you have booked surf lessons with us, we will provide you with all the necessary equipment: board and wetsuit and it is included in the price. You can rent surf equipment with us or bring your own. Life insurance, among other injury factors, is included in the price of classes.
