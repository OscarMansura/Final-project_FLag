Level 1 - Beginner Course

This course is for those of you who have never surfed before, or haven't surfed in a while and wants to repeat the basics: Learn the basics of surfing, get comfortable in the ocean, ride white water waves and start making turns.

Created for beginners

This course is for those of you who have never surfed before, or haven't surfed in a while. and want to repeat the basics. We'll give you a fun and encouraging introduction to surfing, and we'll share the energy when you pick it up. its first waves of white water. Our experienced instructors will ensure you catch as many waves as possible and develop your skills in a safe and pleasant way.

Level 2 - Intermediate Course

Get out to the lineup and surf green waves safely accompanied by the instructor, improve your wave selection and learn to go right and left on a wave and avoid getting caught in the big waves.

Beginners who want to surf green waves

This level is designed for you who have passed our Level 1 or you can catch and surf white water waves on your own. Level 2 will focus on getting to the side outside (line) and surf the waves to the left and to the right. The instructor will create a platform for Encouraging learning with a focus on having as much fun as possible while surfing.

Level 3 - Experienced Course

Get comfortable surfing on your own, without an instructor in the water, improve your surfing skills Spot analysis and wave reading and develop surfing technique and confidence with video training.

Become an independent surfer

This course is designed for you who can ride green waves on your own. own and surf front and back. We will surf soft reefs, point and beach breaks and help you to feel confident surfing most breaks independently. We will also focus on break entry and exit, surf etiquette and safety.
