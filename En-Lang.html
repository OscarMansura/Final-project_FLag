About Us

Ericeira Local Surf School has been teaching surfers from around the globe since 2008. The surf school was established by a local surfer who wanted to share his life long passion for surfing. The main objective of the surf school is not only for you to have an unforgettable experience but to teach you the right way. Our friendly, experienced coaches provide a wealth of knowledge of all aspects of surfing. We are dedicated to share this knowledge of not only board riding technique, but also surf awareness and how to surf safely. No matter what age or size, our accredited surf coaches are trained to be supportive and recognise individual learning levels. Our surf instructors have been trained and accredited by the Portuguese Surf Federation. Short video at right side to see our work, partnership with Nina Movement.

World Surf Reserve

Pedra Branca

The first wave that we find right in front of the Camping of Ericeira is the Stone Branca.Located at the southern tip of Empa Beach, and is named because of a rock submerged, with a lighter tone than the others, located in the zone where waves are normally Caught. It is a very fast left of reef bottom that receives ripples from the quadrant OS to the W / NO quadrant. Due to the shallow bench of the reef that is exposed during the empty tide, Is usually surfed from mid-tide to full tide. Regular and dangerous this wave Is characterized by a zone of rapid starts, followed by a pipe to the inside.

Reef

Continuing along the beach of Empa, which borders a small cliff, we find 300 Meters to North the twin sister of Pedra Branca, a wave called Reef. this right Is formed from a very flat reef plate that develops on land and becomes more Development in the direction of NO. This is another regular and dangerous wave that has a Very short and quick take-off area, followed by a cylindrical tube that ends exactly on the plate Exposed to the inside surface. It only works with undulations of N to NO, with half tide.

Ribeira d'Ilhas

Walking 500 meters to North, there is the most media and cosmopolitan wave of all that integrate the reservation. Located in a valley with a sand beach in the center, where a river flows, its configuration Is a natural amphitheater, ideal for holding surfing events. Not for The first national and international championships held in Portugal. Ribeira d`Ihas is a long right of pointbreak - meaning that the waves follow the contour Of the coast - which receives all kinds of ripples and works on all types of tide, being The most consistent wave in the region.

Cave

This powerful right just started to be surfed Recently, having gained a lot of notoriety in recent years due to To its spectacular and dangers that surrounds. It is extremely radical, tubular And risky. Leaves on a sudden reef plate that does not connect with Earth, located at very low depths. The suction of water from the Wave to its top is so intense that this part below the line Of water - hence its designation - with the pipe that is formed there to become Progressively hollow, wide and flat as the wave approaches Of its end. The Cave must be exclusively used by very experienced surfers.

Crazy Left

A pointbreak that only breaks well with Undulations of N and NO. Being too exposed to the north wind, it is not a very consistent wave In terms of quality, but when it meets the ideal conditions its walls are fast And tubular, with several sections that provide great speeds. Because it is located in a Of drainage of water from the bay, receives only ripples in perfect conditions above 2 meters.

Meet Ericeira

Ericeira is so well known in the surf zone due to the waves, the surfers say they are all different. Is known world wide for its surf breaks and good waves,has a long surfing tradition and every keen surfer, whether beginner or pro, will find epic waves here. Also often used for bodyboarding and SUP. The 1st world surfing reserves in Europe and 2nd in the world is also good enough for swimmers with beaches of good quality. Ericeira is a fantastic party location with several nice bars and one discotheque, one is the oldest of Portugal. The proximity to Lisbon makes it very interesting for people, who like to surf perfect waves during the day and want to party at night.
